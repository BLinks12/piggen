How It Works

Each Pig is uniquely generated from your wallet address and an optional unique ID you provide. Our advanced algorithm ensures every pig is truly one-of-a-kind:

  1. Connect Wallet: Use your Phantom wallet to connect securely to the blockchain.
  2. ID Input: Enter a unique identifier or use your wallet address as the base.
  3. Cryptographic Hashing: Your data transforms into a unique numerical signature.
  4. Trait Generation: This determines breed, colors, personality, accessories, and happiness.
  5. 3D Visualization: Watch your pig come to life in our interactive 3D viewer.

This fusion of blockchain technology and generative art creates a truly personal digital companion that's permanently yours!

Pig Breeds & Traits

Discover the diverse world of digital pigs! Each breed comes with unique characteristics, visual traits, and personality quirks. From noble Hampshires to adorable Pot-bellied companions:

Hampshire

Distinguished black pigs with signature white belts. Known for exceptional meat quality and remarkably calm temperament. These pigs are the aristocrats of the barnyard.

Yorkshire

Majestic white pigs renowned for their nurturing nature and impressive size. Excellent mothers with gentle dispositions, making them beloved farm favorites.

Duroc

Beautiful red-colored pigs with characteristic droopy ears. Hardy survivors with rapid growth rates and notably docile personalities.

Berkshire

Elegant black pigs adorned with distinctive white markings on face, legs, and tail. Producers of premium marbled meat with exceptional flavor.

Landrace

Gracefully long-bodied white pigs with large, expressive droopy ears. Champion mothers known for high milk production and nurturing instincts.

Pot-bellied

Charming small, round pigs originally from Vietnam. Highly intelligent and social creatures that form strong bonds with their human companions.

Tamworth

Golden-red pigs with alert, upright ears. Natural foragers with exceptional bacon-producing qualities and adventurous spirits.

Mangalitsa

The legendary "wooly pig" with distinctive curly hair. Rare heritage breed prized for producing exceptional lard and uniquely flavorful meat.
