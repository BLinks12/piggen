Welcome to Pig Generator 95!

This retro app creates your very own unique pig companion! Here's how it works:

  • Open the App: Double-click the "PigGen95.exe" icon on the desktop.
  • Enter Your Handle: Type your Twitter/X handle (without @) to generate a pig based on it.
  • Add a Seed (Optional): Enter a custom seed for different pig variations.
  • Generate: Click "Generate My Pig" to create your one-of-a-kind pig.
  • View Your Pig: See your pig's 3D sprite, stats, and rarity below.
  • Share: Click "Share on X" to post your pig or "Copy Details" to save its stats.

Have fun creating your digital piggy! Check the Rarity Guide to learn about your pig's exclusivity.
