How It Works

Each Pig is uniquely generated from your wallet address and an optional unique ID you provide. Here's a simple breakdown:

  1. Connect Wallet: Use your Phantom wallet to connect securely.
  2. ID Input: Enter a unique ID or simply use your wallet address.
  3. Hashing Process: Your wallet address and ID transform into a numerical value using our special hashing function.
  4. Attribute Assignment: This determines your Pig's breed, skin color, personality trait, accessory, and "happiness level."
  5. Deterministic Results: The same wallet and ID always adopt the same Pig‚Äîuniquely yours!

This approach blends fun with a touch of code magic, giving you a special ASCII Pig pal tied directly to your wallet and ID!

Pig Breeds & Traits

From playful piglets to wise boars, each pig breed comes with its own special quirks. Here are a few of our ASCII-inspired porcine friends:

Hampshire

Black with a distinctive white belt around the shoulders. Known for excellent meat quality and calm temperament.

Yorkshire

Large white pigs famous for their mothering ability and lean meat. Friendly and easy to handle.

Duroc

Red-colored pigs with droopy ears. Hardy, fast-growing, and known for their docile nature.

Berkshire

Black pigs with white markings on face, legs, and tail. Produces high-quality marbled meat.

Landrace

Long-bodied white pigs with large, droopy ears. Excellent mothers with high milk production.

Pot-bellied

Small, round pigs originally from Vietnam. Intelligent, social, and make great companions.

Tamworth

Golden-red pigs with upright ears. Known for their foraging ability and bacon quality.

Mangalitsa

The "wooly pig" with curly hair. Rare breed known for producing exceptional lard and meat.
